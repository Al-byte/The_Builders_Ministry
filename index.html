Sermons

Discover hope and healing through Christ in our sermon series on overcoming depression. Rooted in Scripture, these messages offer encouragement, practical steps, and spiritual guidance to help you navigate through darkness into God’s light. Whether you're struggling personally or supporting a loved one, find strength in God’s promises, the power of prayer, and the comfort of community. Let His truth renew your mind and restore your joy.

Resources

Explore a variety of resources to help overcome depression through faith. Our collection includes powerful Scriptures, insightful books, articles, and encouraging videos that offer biblical wisdom and practical advice. These materials are designed to support your healing journey, providing both spiritual guidance and emotional comfort. Let these resources strengthen your faith, renew your mind, and remind you of God’s unchanging love and hope, helping you navigate through life's challenges with peace.

Ministry

The Builders Ministry is dedicated to helping individuals overcome depression through the transformative power of faith. We provide biblical guidance, support, and resources to help you find healing and hope in Christ. Through prayer, Scripture, and community, we aim to build emotional resilience and spiritual strength, guiding you from despair to joy. Our mission is to help you rebuild your life, one step at a time, with God’s love as your foundation.

Contact Us

Whether you’re seeking support for overcoming depression, need prayer, or want more information about our resources, we are here for you. Reach out through our website’s contact form, email us directly, or call our office. Our compassionate team is ready to listen, offer guidance, and pray with you. We believe in the power of community and are committed to helping you rebuild your life through Christ’s love and truth. Don’t hesitate to get in touch; we look forward to walking alongside you in your journey of healing and hope. You are never alone—let us support you every step of the way.
