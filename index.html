To Whom It May Concern,

Thank you for this opportunity to introduce The Builders Ministry. We are a 501(c)3 nonprofit ministry founded by Reverend Roger S. Smith of Milwaukee, WI. Our mission is to awaken the sleeping Church and ignite the faith of Christian believers, that they might lead a great Christian revival that grows out of small communities to sweep across the world.

People everywhere are facing different challenges. Despite being a man of faith with a loving family, for more than fifty years, Reverend Smith struggled greatly against depression that brought darkness into his heart. In 2023, our Lord healed Reverend Smith from that sickness and called him back to ministry, to fulfill His will and begin awakening His Church.

Reverend Smith is crafting a series of sermon messages that draw on the Lord’s teaching and the revealed wisdom that healed him from depression. This sermon series is the foundation that we hope to firmly establish and spread to new congregations, through a combination of guest preaching and online outreach through our ministry website.

The Builders Ministry teaches Christians and equips them with the fullness of Christ’s Gospel, to free them from bondage in their lives, and show them the path to repentance and humility. By understanding that they are forgiven for their sins, and embracing their full authority in Jesus, Christian believers become living actions of God that spread His Gospel.

Right now, we are taking the first, tiny steps of a very long journey. Thank you for your prayers, your support, and for sharing in our mission to awaken Christ’s Church.

Your friend in Christ,

Rev. Roger S. Smith
Founder

So they will be called the trees of righteousness [strong and magnificent, distinguished for integrity, justice, and right standing with God], The planting of the Lord, that He may be glorified."

Authority of Scripture

We believe that the Bible is the infallible word of God. “All Scripture is God breathed and is used for teaching, rebuking, correcting, training in righteousness, so that the servant of God may be thoroughly equipped for every good work.” 2 Tim. 3:16-17 The Bible is the truth. “For prophecy never had its origin in the human will, but prophets, though human, spoke from God as they were carried along by the Holy Spirit.” 2 Pet. 1:21.

Nature of God

We believe in the Holy Trinity: God the Father, Jesus the Son and the Holy Spirit. Jesus was born of a virgin birth by the power of the Holy Spirit. Jesus lived on earth and was fully man and fully God. He bore our sins on the cross and was resurrected on the third day. He is alive! Jesus Christ is our salvation, the only way into the Kingdom of heaven.

Path of Salvation

We are saved only by God’s grace through our faith in Jesus Christ. “For it is by grace you have been saved, through faith - and this is not from yourself, it is the gift of God - not by works, so that no one can boast.” Eph. 2:8-9

Sermons

These sermons call the Church to rise from spiritual slumber and step boldly into its divine mission. Discover God’s heart for healing—not just from depression, but from every form of oppression and bondage. Be stirred to bring freedom, hope, and restoration to broken lives. It’s time to awaken, heal, and walk in the power of God’s liberty.

Resources

Discover a rich collection of scriptures, books, articles, and videos designed to awaken the Church to her holy calling. These resources offer truth and encouragement for those burdened by depression, oppression, and spiritual weariness. Rooted in the Word of God, they inspire healing, deliverance, and liberty through Christ. May the Church arise in power, walk in freedom, and shine as a light in these dark days.

Ministry

The Builders Ministry is on fire to awaken the Church from its slumber and unleash a wave of healing and freedom! We stand against depression, oppression, and every burden that holds God’s people captive. By the power of Christ, we break chains, rebuild broken hearts, and ignite a revival of liberty. It’s time to rise, be healed, and walk in the freedom God promised. The Church will roar with victory—let’s build it together!

Contact Us

Whether you’re seeking support for overcoming depression, need prayer, or want more information about our resources, we are here for you. Reach out through our website’s contact form, email us directly, or call our office. Our compassionate team is ready to listen, offer guidance, and pray with you. We believe in the power of community and are committed to helping you rebuild your life through Christ’s love and truth. Don’t hesitate to get in touch; we look forward to walking alongside you in your journey of healing and hope. You are never alone—let us support you every step of the way.
