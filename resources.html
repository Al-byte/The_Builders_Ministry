Resource Center

The Resource Center is a dynamic digital collection designed to help revive and reawaken the Church. It offers a wealth of books, teachings, and other resources for study, prayer, and spiritual growth. Covering key topics such as theology, discipleship, Christian living, and ministry, this library serves as a powerful tool to strengthen faith, deepen understanding of biblical truths, and equip believers for impactful service in Christ.

Scriptures

These scriptures are a battle cry to awaken the Church and break every chain of oppression. Rooted in God’s truth, they carry the power to bring freedom, healing, and deliverance. This section has been prepared to equip you for spiritual battle—so you can rise in power, walk in liberty, and break every bondage in Jesus’ name.

Books

Books

Battlefield of the Mind

Battlefield of the Mind by Joyce Meyer explores the power of thoughts, emphasizing how negative thinking can hinder personal growth. It offers practical advice for transforming your mindset to live a victorious, fulfilled life.

Broken Children, Grown-Up Pain

Broken Children, Grown-Up Pain by Paul Hegstrom explores how childhood trauma shapes adult behavior. It offers insight into emotional wounds, abuse cycles, and the healing process through faith, therapy, and personal responsibility, guiding readers toward restoration and healthy relationships.

Heaven

Heaven by Randy Alcorn offers a biblical exploration of the afterlife, describing the nature of heaven, its pleasures, and purpose. It aims to provide readers with a hopeful and accurate understanding of eternal life with God.

Power Thoughts

Power Thoughts by Joyce Meyer emphasizes the impact of thoughts on personal well-being and spiritual growth. It encourages transforming negative thinking patterns into positive, faith-filled thoughts to improve life and achieve inner peace.

The Pursuit of Holiness

The Pursuit of Holiness by Jerry Bridges emphasizes the importance of striving for holiness in the Christian life. It explores the role of God's grace and personal effort in living a godly, obedient life.

Audio

Our Audio Teachings library offers powerful, Scripture-centered messages designed to bring hope, healing, and spiritual renewal. Each teaching is grounded in biblical truth, addressing the struggles of the mind and heart with the transforming power of God’s Word. Whether you’re seeking freedom from mental and emotional battles or a deeper passion for Christ, these messages are meant to inspire lasting change.

Bright and Morning Star

Jesus, the Bright Morning Star, shines brilliantly as the Root and Offspring of David, bringing hope, light, and victory. He overcomes darkness and grants believers authority, wisdom, and eternal dominion with righteous power.

Exodus 5

This teaching on Exodus 5 explores Moses’ first encounter with Pharaoh, revealing the spiritual resistance that often follows God’s call. It highlights how trials and opposition can deepen faith, strengthen obedience, and prepare believers for God’s deliverance.

Heaven and Other Faiths

This teaching examines other religions’ views on heaven—Buddhism rejects heaven for rebirth, Islam promises paradise through works, Mormonism teaches layered heavens, Hinduism seeks liberation—but only Christianity offers eternal heaven by God’s grace through faith in Jesus Christ.

Jehovah Jireh

God as Jehovah Jireh, our Provider, reveals faith through trials like Abraham’s sacrifice. Trusting God amid pain strengthens faith, deepens worship, and assures provision, reflecting God’s eternal care and calling us to obedient dependence.

The Lion of The Tribe of Judah

Jesus, the Lion of Judah, embodies kingly authority, power, and victory. He fulfills prophecy as sovereign ruler who triumphs over sin, combining majesty and sacrifice, offering salvation and eternal reign as God’s conquering King.

Psalm 37

Psalm 37 contrasts the fleeting success of the wicked with the lasting inheritance of the righteous who trust, delight, and commit to God—promising God’s protection, reward, restoration, and encouragement to proclaim His gospel faithfully.

Psalm 40

In Psalm 40, David shares a powerful testimony of God’s rescue and restoration. This teaching unpacks the psalm’s message of hope, gratitude, and the call to proclaim God’s faithfulness to others.

Psalm 96

Psalm 96 calls all creation to joyfully worship God’s glory, proclaim His salvation, and honor His righteous judgment. It invites believers to rejoice in His reign as King and trust His justice over the earth.

Psalm 103

Psalm 103 calls our whole being to praise God for His abundant mercy, forgiveness, healing, and steadfast love. He renews strength like an eagle, delivers us from despair, and blesses us with everlasting compassion.

Ruling on The New Earth

Through faith in Jesus, believers are justified to inherit and rule God’s eternal Kingdom on the New Earth—fulfilling humanity’s original purpose to reign with Christ, exercising joyful authority, responsibility, and expanding God’s kingdom forever.
